2. Description of Service

Break Speed Coach is a training and tracking tool that estimates pool break speed from audio recorded through your device microphone. It is designed to help players measure and improve their break over time.

  • Player profile management with persistent break history.
  • Session summaries, trend charts, and consistency metrics.
