What We Measure

This app estimates break speed from the sound of your break shot — no extra hardware needed.

It detects the cue-ball strike and rack scatter in your phone's microphone to calculate impact speed.

Results are confidence-based estimates, not radar-gun measurements. Use them as training feedback.

◐
Phone Placement

Place your phone flat on the rail near the rack, mic side facing the table.

  • Same spot every attempt for consistent readings
  • Stable surface only — not handheld or leaning
  • Don't touch the phone during recording
  • Record 3 breaks per session for the best read
●
Understanding Confidence

Every reading gets a confidence tier based on how clearly the impact was detected.

● HIGH Strongest reading — trust this number
◐ NEAR-HIGH Very usable — use with context
◑ MEDIUM Rough estimate — interpret carefully
○ LOW Retake suggested when possible
